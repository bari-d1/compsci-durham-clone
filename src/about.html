About Us


We are a growing Department, ranked in the top 10 in the Complete University Guide 2021.

We are proud of our high-quality teaching and research and the people who make it all happen. We strive to
be a friendly and inclusive place to study and work for all.


We are one of Durhamâ€™s newest academic departments, having split from the School of Engineering and
Computer Science in 2017, we have rapidly grown to be a thriving home for staff, undergraduates,
postgraduates, and visitors.

We are located in the beautiful city of Durham and are now set up in a brand new purpose-built
building along with the Department of Mathematical Sciences.

We offer a range of Programmes, our undergraduate degree is available as either a 3 year BSc
or a 4 year MEng and contribute to three taught postgraduate courses: Scientific Computing (MISCADA), Business
Analytics, and Data Science. We also have a thriving research culture involving faculty, postdoctoral
researchers, and postgraduate research students.

Our research strengths include theoretical computer science, computer vision, AI, high-performance &
scientific computing, digital humanities, health, and networks. And our strong alumni network contributes to
the department in many ways - including our External Advisory Board, visiting speakers, student
placements, and philanthropic support of scholarships. We have some hugely influential alumni in the Tech
Sector.
